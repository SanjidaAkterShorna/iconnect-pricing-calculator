Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I exceed my included minutes? ▼

You'll be billed at the per-minute overage rate shown on your plan. There are no surprise charges — you can monitor usage in real time from your dashboard.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime? ▼

Yes. You can switch plans at any time. When upgrading, you'll be prorated for the remaining billing period. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial? ▼

We offer a guided demo where our team configures a live AI agent for your business. Contact sales to schedule yours.

What's included in the one-time setup fee? ▼

The setup fee covers AI agent configuration, voice selection, script development, CRM connection, and a live training session with your team.

Do you offer custom enterprise pricing? ▼

Absolutely. For volumes above 50,000 minutes or custom requirements, contact our sales team for a tailored quote.
